— product grid (union of all active DCs) —
Pills are the buyer’s saved DCs (distributor in orange + the DC). They persist as the default and reset to all-on every login — the buyer never re-selects. With all on, the catalog is the union of products across all three DCs. Tapping a pill is a toggle — the only thing it does. Add or Remove DC opens the management panel where the buyer adds a new DC (with its account number) or removes one they no longer use; their last DC can’t be removed (floor of 1). Show Discovery (purple, set apart, default off) is a different kind of toggle — it isn’t a DC. Turning it on adds products from beyond the buyer’s DCs into the view, clearly marked and non-orderable (pre-commit / sample / wishlist only). Off by default so the normal shop stays purely orderable.

Header at a glance

  • Nav links: Shop · Reorder · New · Sale · Brands — the ways buyers actually navigate (product-first, not DC-first).
  • Show Discovery = a separate lens (purple, default off) for products beyond the buyer’s DCs — non-orderable, pre-commit/sample/wishlist only. Not a DC.
  • DC pills = the buyer’s saved distribution centers; persist as default, all-on at every login.
  • Tap a pill = toggle its products in/out of the view. Toggling one off just shows what’s available from the remaining DCs; nothing is removed, the cart is untouched.
  • Add or Remove DC = opens the management panel to add a new DC (with account number) or remove one no longer used (last DC can’t be removed).
  • Right side: search, account menu (B2B links), cart with count.
