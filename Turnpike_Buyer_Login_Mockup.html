First login after approval only. The buyer registered with one DC; this one-time prompt invites the rest. It reuses the same selector + account-number modal as “Add a DC”. Skippable — a returning buyer never sees it and lands straight on the shop above.

What happens on login

  • DCs hydrate automatically — saved (distributor, DC, account #) load into the session; no re-selecting.
  • First time: a skippable prompt to add more DCs. Every time after: straight to the shop.
  • Catalog is scoped to the union of products at their held DCs — they only see what they can order.
  • Navigate by product — reorders first, then category / brand / search. DC pills are view toggles, not navigation; the last DC can’t be removed.
  • Cards follow WS3 — wholesale-led pricing, SRP benchmark, deal strike, inline routing for single-DC buyers.
